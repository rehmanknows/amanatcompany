About

Amanat Ali Ghulam

The General Manager of the metal and wood furniture factory Mr. Amanat Ali
Ghulam founded the factory in 2002.
Amanat Ali Ghulam metal & wood factory company gathered so many professional and experienced experts, in addition to the latest modern machineries in furniture and decoration filed to guarantee that we will be able to provide best products to satisfy all our customers with the best international standards and a real warranty, to be best in furniture and decoration filed. We always seek to provide best specifications with lowest price.

region.
